Your Safety is Our Priority

Guidelines for the safe handling and usage of LPG at home.

In Case of Gas Leakage (Smell of Gas)

DON'T switch on/off any electrical switches or fans.

DO open all doors and windows for ventilation.

DO turn off the cylinder regulator immediately.

DON'T light matches or lighters.

Call Emergency Helpline:1906

Storage Rules

  • Always keep the cylinder in a vertical position.
  • Store cylinders in a well-ventilated area at ground level.
  • Keep cylinders away from heat sources and direct sunlight

Maintenance

  • Check the rubber tube (Suraksha Hose) for cracks every 6 months.
  • Replace the regulator every 5 years with an authorized one.
  • Always use BIS-approved stoves and tubes.
